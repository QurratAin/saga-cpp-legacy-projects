\section{Summary}
We would to request supplemental allocation on TeraGrid resources. In the first year of our multi--year allocation (TG-MCB090174) we requested 5 million SUs on ranger and 3 million SUs on Kraken. Our allocation was awarded half the requested SUs on ranger (2.5 million) and 2 out of 3 million SUs on Kraken. As expected, we ran out of SUs on ranger half way through the year and are running quite low on SUs on Kraken.

We kindly request 1.5 million SUs on Kraken and 1 million SUs on ranger to tide us over to the next allocation renewal cycle. As it stands, our research will stall in the first week of April and remain that way for several months.
