Filesystem e.g. /home/oweider
=============================
Device total space: 		df /home/oweidner | awk '/\// {print $2}'
Device available space:		df /home/oweidner | awk '/\// {print $4}'
Quota used 			quota | awk '/home/ {print $2}'
Quota Limit			quota | awk '/home/ {print $4}'

Cluster queue e.g. workq on LONI
================================
Queue total nodes		qfree | awk '/workq/ {print $4}' | tr -d ,
Queue free nodes		qfree | awk '/workq/ {print $6}' | tr -d ,
